About this challenge

The Syrian refugee population is disproportionately composed of women, many of whom have been widowed or separated from their families and are often struggling to support children. The United Nations High Commission on Refugees (UNHCR) has called attention to the plight of hundreds of thousands of Syrian refugee women who are not only facing difficult living conditions but also sexual and gender basewd violence (SGBV).

Develop a plan to address SGBV in Cesar 1, an informal camp outside Zahle in the Bekaa Valley.
